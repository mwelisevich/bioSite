Taylor Welisevich

Growing up in St. Paul, Minnesota, Taylor has experienced a rich urban environment while also the pleasures of midwestern life. She and her husband spent time living on the West Coast before returning to the Midwest where they lived in Omaha, Nebraska for two years before embarking on the biggest adventure of their lives when they moved to England, UK in April. Taylor and her husband have visited London a few times and thoroughly enjoyed all their trips. Taylor Absolutely loves all things Disney. When she and her husband moved to California, she knew she could fulfill her lifelong dream of visiting Disneyland. Within two years, she would check off another goal and visit Disneyworld. Next on the list is Disneyland Paris.

Moving to Europe has been an exciting change, but Taylor and her family did say goodbye to their home in Nebraska, selling their first home only a year after they first purchased it. One cannot discuss Taylor without mentioning her two dogs. Taylor has a Border Collie/German Sheppard named Zena that she and her husband rescued when they lived in Seaside, California. Her other dog is a rascal named Elwood who is a Pembroke Welsh Corgi they picked up during their time in Nebraska. The dogs are fitting in perfectly in England, having met countless friends at the dog parks in the area.
